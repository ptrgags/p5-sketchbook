Conformal Geometric Algebra (CGA) Test (2026-02)

I want to port some of the things I did in math-notebook to p5 so I can animate them. Except this time, I want to try using 2D Conformal Geometric Algebra (2D CGA) rather than complex numbers. There's a lot of moving pieces (see the GitHubIssue). So far this is just a test of rendering the geometry primitives. A Cline is a generalized circle or line. It does also have the corner case of a circle of radius zero (aka a null point in CGA).

So far, this sketch creates and renders Cline objects. I also started implementing the key math operations such as geometric product and sandwich product. Here, I'm using this math to take the original clines (yellow shapes) and transform them. The blue shapes are the original ones flipped over the yellow line. The orange shapes are the original ones under circle inversion in the yellow circle.
