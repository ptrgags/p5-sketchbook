Conformal Geometric Algebra (CGA) Test (2026-02)

I want to port some of the things I did in math-notebook to p5 so I can animate them. Except this time, I want to try using 2D Conformal Geometric Algebra (2D CGA) rather than complex numbers. There's a lot of moving pieces (see the GitHubIssue). So far this is just a test of rendering the geometry primitives. A Cline is a generalized circle or line. Points are encoded as null points (circles with radius 0).

This sketch creates some Cline and NullPoint objects and transforms them with versors. The original shapes are in yellow. The blue shapes are the originals flipped over the yellow line. The orange shapes are the originals inverted in the yellow circle. The green animated shapes are the originals rotated around the enter of the screen.
