Animated Tangle (2025-12/2026-01)

This sketch is a doodle in the same style as how I draw on paper. However, here I can animate the patterns!

Animation Breakdown

The following is a short description of each animation in the doodle. For further details, trivia and concept art, see the README.

IMG: Diagram of the tangle with panels labeled

  • Top Level: Animated Tangle
    • Panel 1: Seascape A scene of mountains and ocean waves scan across the scene.
    • Panel 2: Traffic Platforms scroll across the screen, but get bunched up like a traffic jam.
    • Panel 3: Quadrants
      • Panel 3A: Hitomezashi A sashiko embroidery pattern with animated stitches.
      • Panel 3B: Circle Fan Circles that fan out from the corner of the panel.
      • Panel 3C: Peek Everything is better with googly eyes.
      • Panel 3D: Brick Wall A wall, built brick by brick.
    • Panel 4: Swaying Coral A coral, gently swaying in the current.
      • Panel 4A: Coral Polyps A stylized representation of the coral polyps. The tentacles retract quickly as if in response to danger, and cautiously open again.
    • Panel 5: Geode An agate geode growing layer by layer.
    • Panel 6: Barber Pole and Doors
      • Panel 6A: Barber Pole Moving stripes like on the pole of a barber shop.
      • Panel 6B: Doors A set of doors that open and shut like jaws.
