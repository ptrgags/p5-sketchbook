Waking Hours Clock (2026-06)

This clock is less about telling time, and more about understanding one's sleep/wake cycle.

For this first iteration, the clock is hard-coded to my own (ideal) sleep schedule. I try to allow 8 hours for sleep, and I wake up at 6 AM.

One satisfying result of this is there are 16 waking hours, which is a power of 2. This means I can divide my day into 4 quarters (Delineated by green ticks). One last tick mark divides the night into "late night" and "early morning" portions.
