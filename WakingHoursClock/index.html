Waking Hours Clock (2026-06/2026-07)

Controls: Drag the handles to adjust the sleep/wake times. You can also drag the bezel to adjust both times by the same amount.

This clock visualizes one's sleep/wake cycle. The orange arc represents waking hours, while the purple arc represents sleeping hours.

Here time is labeled in an unusual way. The blue tick marks next to the orange arc represent the fraction of the time between waking and sleeping. Notice that adjusting the sleep/wake times may cause these tick marks to get closer together or further apart.

The sleeping hours work a bit differently. The night is divided into two equal halves. "Early Morning" is the half before waking up, and gets a negative fraction. This is to be interpreted as "fraction of the night before the day." Meanwhile, "Late Evening" is the half after the sleep time are marked with a plus sign. This is to be interpreted as "fraction of the night after the day."
