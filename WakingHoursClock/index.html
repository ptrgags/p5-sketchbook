Waking Hours Clock (2026-06)

Controls: Drag the handles to adjust the sleep/wake times. You can also drag the bezel to adjust both times by the same amount.

This clock visualizes one's sleep/wake cycle. The orange arc represents waking hours, while the purple arc represents sleeping hours.

The blue tick marks represent subdivisions of one's day. For waking hours, I divide it into halves, quarters, eights, and 16ths. The length of these divisions will vary as the sleep/wake times change.

For the sleeping hours, I first divide them into 2 portions. Half for "early morning" and half for "late night". Each is divided into quarters.
