12 Major Keys (2026-04)

Diagram of the 12 keys on piano, but color coded to highlight the scale structure. In particular:

  • Red keys - The tonic (first note) of the major scale
  • White keys - The other notes of the major scale
  • Black keys - Sharps and flats. These are not a part of the major scale.

To play a major scale in a given key, start on that key's tonic (red), then play only the white and red keys above it, in order from left to right.
