Bézier (2016)

Click and drag the canvas to move the control curve. Scroll the mouse wheel to change how many tangent lines are drawn.

I made this in college when I was learning about Bézier curves and other splines. There are several ways to make a Bézier curve, I chose to use the one where you interpolate pairs of control points, then interpolate the interpolated points, and so on, until you get a single point on the curve. This is called De Casteljau's Algorithm but I did not know that until quite a bit later.
