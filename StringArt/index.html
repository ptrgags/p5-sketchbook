This sketch visualizes modular integer arithmetic. This is your usual addition and multiplication, except wrapped around a circle. If a number is larger than the number of points on the circle, you just wrap around to 0. It's just like going around the numbers of a clock or spaces on a board game.

The visualization works as follows: take a point on the circle. Apply the selected math function to transform it into a new number. Then draw a line between the original and new points. Doing this for all points on the circle creates a web structure that looks like string art.

Sometimes a number will land back on itself. In cases like these, a circle is drawn around the point to emphasize that it stays in place.

The "Animate" option animates the transition from a number to its transformed value. This makes it easier to tell which end of a line is which. More importantly, it creates mesmerizing patterns!
